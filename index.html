Our Process

We are a company with a conscience.

The belief that software can provide a profound public good is part of our DNA. That's why devote time to work on our open source projects like WVFindWater and CheckUp.

In January of 2014, over 300,000 West Virginians were left without water when a storage facility leaked several thousand gallons of a coal-processing chemical upstream from a water treatment plant. We realized there was no central resource for finding clean water distribution centers, so we made one.
